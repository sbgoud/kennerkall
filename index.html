How It Works

Kennerkall makes consultations easy for both customers and professionals.

1. Browse & Choose

Pick from a range of professionals with clear **per-minute** rates.

2. Load Your Wallet

Add funds using any payment method. Your balance covers per-minute calls.

3. Call & Pay by the Minute
